Atchi Muite Hoi

Web Design

A game to win Atchi Muite Hoi forever. Atchi Muite Hoi is a Japanese game played with two players. One person moves their head in any of the four directions (up, down, right, left) while the other player points their ginger at the same time to guess which direction the other player moved in. If the player guesses correctly, they win. Photo credits to its owners.
